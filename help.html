❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Log in to your Vastraa account and go to My Orders. Click on the order ID to see real-time tracking status. You'll also receive SMS & email updates at every stage.

What is the return policy?

We offer a 15-day hassle-free return window from the date of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, with all original tags intact. Initiate a return from your account dashboard.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available at an additional charge of ₹99 for most metro cities.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placing them. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Contact us immediately if needed.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after the returned item reaches our warehouse. The amount is credited to your original payment method or Vastraa Wallet.

Are the products authentic and handmade?

Yes! Every product on Vastraa is sourced directly from verified artisans and weavers. We conduct quality checks and provide authenticity certificates for handloom products.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we ship across India. International shipping to UAE, UK, USA, Canada, and Australia is available for select products. Check product pages for availability.

How do I apply a coupon code?

Add items to your cart and proceed to the Cart page. You'll find the "Have a Coupon Code?" section where you can enter and apply your code before checkout.
